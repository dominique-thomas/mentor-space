Mentor Space is a guided leadership simulation designed to help new and aspiring leaders explore their personal leadership style through reflection and conversation.

This project is free to use and is provided as-is. The website and resources were designed and developed by Dominique Thomas.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) responses powered by Gemini and hosted via Firebase. Illustrations and AI prompt logic were developed with assistance using ChatGPT.
